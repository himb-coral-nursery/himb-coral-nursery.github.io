    <h1 class="page-title">Our History</h1>

    <p>The HIMB Coral Nursery began as an effort to recover corals of opportunity growing on decommissioned fish pens at HIMB lead by Drs. Zac Forsman and Ingrid Knapp in 2017. </p>
    <p>After success of the initial pilot, the nursery expanded significantly with new docks in 2025 to its current status. For more, visit our <a href="/history/" style="color: #C2E5DF;">HIMB Coral Nursery history</a> page</p>
