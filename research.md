<h1 class="page-title">Long-term Monitoring</h1>

<div class="full-width-section" markdown="1">

The nursery is photographed bimonthly to generate 3D models and orthomosaics of each rack. This allows us to track coral health over time, measure growth rates, and identify bleaching or disease events. When combined with environmental data, researchers can select corals with known stress responses to better predict how reefs will respond to future climate events.
